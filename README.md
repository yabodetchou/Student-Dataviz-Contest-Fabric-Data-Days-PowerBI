## Goal
This report provides a comprehensive analysis to help students and families evaluate colleges based on ROI (Return on Investment), career outcomes, and student life factors.

## Findings

### 1. College Finder Dashboard Page 
This page provides the initial filtering metrics and shows the overall landscape of the available colleges:

* Cost Range: Annual tuition varies widely, with the most expensive college charging $9,500 (Crystal Coast Academy, C008), which is $4,500 more than the most affordable option at $5,000 (Ancient Ruins College, C010).

* School Size: Student enrollment ranges from a small, focused campus of 1,200 students (Crystal Coast Academy) up to a very large institution of 4,000 students (Ancient Ruins College).

* Overall Quality (MagicScore): The top-ranked college (Crystal Coast Academy) holds a MagicScore of 98, while the lowest-scoring college (Ancient Ruins College) is at 70, highlighting a 28-point spread in composite quality.

* Admission Difficulty: Colleges are grouped relatively evenly, with 40% of the institutions requiring a 'Hard' admission level.


### 2. ROI & Career Outcomes Page

This page focuses on the economic return of an education, comparing cost versus future earnings:

* Highest Earning Potential: Graduates of Crystal Coast Academy (C008) command the highest average starting salary at $72,000, which is $24,000 higher than the lowest-earning college in the dataset.

* Placement Success: The highest graduate placement rate is 97% (Crystal Coast Academy), while the lowest is 80%, showing a 17 percentage point difference in immediate career success.

* Program-Level ROI: Program analysis reveals that the Trade Apprenticeship (Apprenticeship level, Easy Difficulty) is highly competitive, offering an average starting salary up to $76,693, closely rivaling the top Graduate programs like Advanced Elemental Sorcery (up to $77,580). This suggests vocational paths offer comparable financial returns to higher degrees.


### 3. Student Life & Vibe Check Page (Fit Analysis)

This page quantifies non-academic metrics that define the campus experience:

* Overall Happiness: Student satisfaction is generally high, with scores ranging from 4.0 to a perfect 5.0 (Crystal Coast Academy) across all institutions.

* Social Engagement: The number of available Clubs and Organizations varies from a low of 18 at one institution to a high of 45 at Stormwind Academy, indicating diverse levels of student engagement.

* Facilities and Support: Silverleaf University of Healing (C003) boasts top-tier ratings, with perfect 5.0 scores for both Housing Quality and Facilities Rating, along with a high 4.9 Overall Happiness score.
